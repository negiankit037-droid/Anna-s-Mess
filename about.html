Our Legacy

TRADITION IN EVERY BITE

The Humble Beginnings

Anna’s Mess was born not just as a restaurant, but as a vision to bring the soulful flavors of South India to the heart of Rishikesh. The word "Anna" in Tamil translates to "Elder Brother"—a title that carries the weight of responsibility, care, and a sense of belonging. We treat every guest as a member of our own family.

"We don't just fill stomachs; we feed the soul with the heritage of Tamil Nadu and Kerala."

The Secret of Our Kitchen

What makes our food distinct? It’s our refusal to take shortcuts. Every morning, our chefs begin the day by hand-selecting the finest spices, which are then ground to perfection using techniques that have existed for centuries. From our signature Filter Coffee to our Ghee Podi Masala, everything is made from scratch.

We prioritize health as much as taste, incorporating ancient grains like Ragi and the natural richness of fresh Coconut Milk into our menu. No artificial colors, no MSG—just pure, honest cooking.
